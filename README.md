# Pharmacy Sales & Purchase Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project is an Excel-based Business Intelligence Dashboard developed to analyze pharmacy sales and purchase performance for FY 2023–2024.

The dashboard provides insights into:
- Revenue & Profitability
- Monthly & Quarterly Trends
- Customer Performance
- Supplier Analysis
- Product Category Analysis
- GST & Tax Reporting
- Staff Performance Tracking

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Advanced Formulas
- Data Visualization
- KPI Dashboard Design

---

## Key Business Insights
- Revenue reached ₹71.5L across 2 years
- Profit margin declined due to increased purchase costs
- Respiratory category contributed 25.6% of total sales
- Top 2 suppliers contributed 40% of purchases
- Walk-in customers generated 16.9% of revenue
